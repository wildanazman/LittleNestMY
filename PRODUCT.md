# Product

## Register

product

## Users

LittleNest MY is used by parents and caregivers, often tired, busy, and on mobile, who need to record baby care and mama wellbeing quickly without feeling overwhelmed.

## Product Purpose

LittleNest MY helps families track baby care, health, appointments, milestones, Mama Care, breast pumping, and family handoff information in one reliable product. Success means parents can log the right thing in seconds, trust that real data is synced, and understand what needs attention next.

## Brand Personality

Calm, premium, reassuring, and parent-friendly. The interface should feel polished and Apple-like while staying practical for sleep-deprived users.

## Anti-references

Do not make the app feel like a prototype, fake-data demo, noisy tracker, medical chart, generic SaaS dashboard, or decorative landing page. Avoid cluttered lists, confusing hidden actions, unreadable dark mode, broken back navigation, and UI that looks unfinished.

## Design Principles

1. Make the next useful action obvious.
2. Keep logging fast enough for one-handed mobile use.
3. Separate major workflows into focused pages instead of crowding one screen.
4. Use calm premium surfaces, readable type, and consistent LittleNest theming.
5. Preserve trust: real user data, clear sync behavior, and no mock content in production flows.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Prioritize mobile touch targets, clear contrast in light and dark mode, reduced-motion support, readable text, semantic controls, and forgiving flows for tired parents. Aim for WCAG AA contrast for product UI.
